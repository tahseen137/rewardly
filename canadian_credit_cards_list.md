## Notes

1. **Card Changes**: The credit card market changes frequently with new launches, retirements, and rebranding. This list represents cards available as of January 2026.

2. **Business vs Personal**: Many banks offer parallel business card versions (not all listed individually above).

3. **Regional Variations**: Some cards may be available only to specific credit unions or regional financial institutions.

4. **US Dollar Cards**: Many banks offer US dollar versions of their primary cards (e.g., Scotiabank U.S. Dollar Visa).

5. **Network Tiers**: Most Visa and Mastercard cards come in multiple tiers (Classic, Gold, Platinum, World Elite) with different benefits.

6. **Partner Programs**: Several cards partner with loyalty programs (Aeroplan, Marriott, AIR MILES, Scene+, Avion, Aventura).
