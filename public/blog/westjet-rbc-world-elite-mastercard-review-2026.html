Card Overview

The WestJet RBC World Elite Mastercard is the flagship co-branded airline card for WestJet — one of Canada's two main carriers. Unlike most Canadian travel cards that earn transferable points (Aeroplan, Scene+, MR), this card earns WestJet Dollars — a cash-value currency redeemable exclusively on WestJet flights and WestJet Vacations packages.

The card's headline benefit is the annual companion voucher: once per year, you can bring a travel companion on a WestJet flight for a base fare of just $119 round-trip (plus taxes and fees). For a couple who flies WestJet at least once per year, this voucher alone often exceeds the $119 annual fee.

Is the WestJet RBC World Elite Worth It?

Yes — if you fly WestJet at least once per year as a couple or family. The companion voucher alone typically delivers $200–$400 in savings on domestic and sun destination flights, covering the $119 fee with room to spare. Add free checked bags for your whole travel group and you're looking at $300–$600 in annual value from perks alone.

No — if you're a flexible traveller or rarely fly WestJet. The 1.5% earn rate outside WestJet purchases is competitive but not class-leading, and WestJet Dollars are completely locked to one airline. If you want flexibility — Aeroplan, Air Miles, transferable points — look at the Amex Cobalt, TD Aeroplan, or Scotia Gold instead.

The ideal cardholder: Someone who takes at least one WestJet trip per year (especially to sun destinations or cross-country), travels with a partner or family, and values simplicity (WestJet Dollars are dead simple to use — no seat restrictions, no blackout dates on award redemptions).

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use the WestJet RBC companion voucher on any WestJet flight?
You can use the companion voucher on any WestJet-operated round-trip flight, including domestic, transborder (US), and sun destinations. It does not apply to WestJet Rewards redemptions, premium cabins (WestJet Business), or flights operated by partner airlines on code-share routes. The voucher is valid for one full year from issuance — don't let it expire unused, as it cannot be reissued.
Does the WestJet RBC card work at Costco Canada?
Yes — the WestJet RBC World Elite Mastercard is accepted at Costco Canada (Costco accepts Mastercard). You'll earn 1.5% WestJet Dollars on Costco purchases, the same as all non-WestJet spend. This is a reasonable general earn rate for Costco shopping, though the PC Financial World Elite Mastercard (PC Optimum) and HSBC World Elite Mastercard offer competitive alternatives.
What's the income requirement for the WestJet RBC World Elite Mastercard?
The WestJet RBC World Elite Mastercard requires a minimum personal income of $80,000 or household income of $150,000. If you don't meet the income requirement, RBC offers the WestJet RBC Mastercard (no World Elite) with a $39 annual fee — that card offers 1.5% WestJet Dollars on WestJet purchases and 1% everywhere else, plus an annual companion voucher at $99 base fare (slightly less valuable but still worthwhile for WestJet flyers).
