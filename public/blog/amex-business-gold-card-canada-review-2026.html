The Core Feature: Auto-Rotating 4x Categories

The Amex Business Gold's defining feature is its automatic category system. Each month, the card looks at your spending across 6 eligible categories and applies the 4x multiplier to your top 2 categories — no registration required, no manual selection. This makes it uniquely suited for businesses with variable monthly spending patterns.

No category cap: Unlike Amex Cobalt (which caps 5x grocery/dining earn at $30K/year), the Business Gold has NO stated cap on the 4x earn rate for its automatic categories. High-volume spenders — businesses running $5,000–$20,000/month through eligible categories — earn proportionally. This is a key distinction from most Canadian rewards cards.

Our Verdict: 4.3 / 5 — Excellent for Business Owners with Diverse Spend

The Amex Business Gold is one of the most versatile business credit cards in Canada. The auto-rotating 4x categories mean it self-optimizes as your monthly spend shifts — a killer feature for project-based businesses or consultants with variable expense patterns. The $100 travel credit makes the effective cost just $99/year, which any business spending $2,000+/month on eligible categories will recoup quickly. Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use the Amex Business Gold as a sole proprietor or freelancer?
Yes — you don't need an incorporated business to apply. Sole proprietors, freelancers, consultants, and side-business operators are eligible. Apply as a self-employed individual. Your personal income counts toward the income requirement. The "business" requirement is flexible — any professional earning income outside of traditional employment qualifies.
How does the automatic category selection actually work?
At the end of each billing period, Amex totals your spending across the 6 eligible categories and identifies your top 2 by dollar volume. Those two categories get the 4x multiplier applied retroactively for that month. You don't need to do anything — Amex handles the math automatically. If you tie in any categories, Amex breaks the tie in a standard way (their terms specify the tiebreaker order).
Is the Amex Business Gold the same as the personal Amex Gold Canada?
No — these are different products. The personal Amex Gold (not widely available in Canada) has different benefits. The Amex Business Gold is a dedicated small business card with business-oriented categories (shipping, advertising, tech). The Amex Cobalt is the most popular personal Amex in Canada and is the closest personal equivalent in terms of rewards flexibility.
