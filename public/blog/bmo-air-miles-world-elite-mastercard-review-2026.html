Verdict — Top Card for AIR MILES Collectors, Average for General Use

The BMO Air Miles WE MC is the best co-branded AIR MILES card in Canada, with 3 miles/$25 at partners and 4 DragonPass lounge visits. However, outside AIR MILES partners, the earn rate (1 mile/$25 = 0.42%) is poor by modern standards. Best used as a category card alongside a strong general-spend card. If you're not a committed AIR MILES collector, the BMO eclipse or Scotia Gold earn more on day-to-day spending.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the BMO Air Miles World Elite earn miles at Costco?
Yes — Costco Canada accepts Mastercard, and the BMO Air Miles WE MC earns 1 mile per $25 at Costco (the "all other purchases" category). Costco does not qualify for the 2 miles/$25 grocery bonus because Costco is coded as MCC 5300 (Wholesale Club), not MCC 5411 (grocery). For Costco shopping, the PC Financial WE MC or Rogers Red WE MC earn a better effective rate with no annual fee.
What is the income requirement for the BMO Air Miles World Elite Mastercard?
The BMO Air Miles World Elite Mastercard requires a minimum individual income of $80,000 or household income of $150,000. If you don't meet this threshold, BMO offers the BMO Air Miles Mastercard (no-fee) and BMO Air Miles Mastercard for World credit (lower income requirement) with similar earn structures but without the premium benefits like DragonPass access and the higher 3 miles/$25 partner earn rate.
