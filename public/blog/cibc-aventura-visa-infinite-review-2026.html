The CIBC Aventura Visa Infinite is CIBC's flexible travel card — unlike the Aeroplan card (locked to Air Canada), Aventura Points can be redeemed for any flight on any airline with no blackout dates, no seat restrictions, and at any price. At $139/year with 4 Priority Pass lounge visits and a solid earn rate on everyday categories, it's a strong mid-tier travel card for Canadians who fly multiple airlines or value redemption flexibility over Aeroplan maximization.

Aventura Points — The Key Differentiator

Aventura Points can be redeemed for any flight, on any airline, at any published fare — Air Canada, WestJet, Sunwing, Porter, Air Transat, Delta, United, British Airways, and hundreds more. No partner-airline restrictions, no blackout dates, no minimum seat inventory. This flexibility is the card's main advantage over co-branded airline cards.

Points are applied against the dollar cost of the ticket in the CIBC Rewards portal. The value you get per point depends on which ticket you book — higher-priced tickets give you more value per point than base-fare seats. The sweet spot is mid-range tickets where cash prices are high enough to make point redemption worthwhile.

Priority Pass Lounge Access

4 complimentary Priority Pass lounge visits per year. Priority Pass provides access to 1,300+ airport lounges globally including Plaza Premium lounges at Toronto Pearson, Vancouver, and other major Canadian airports. After 4 free visits, additional visits cost $32 USD each. Perfect for occasional travellers who want lounge access without paying for a premium card.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between CIBC Aventura and CIBC Aeroplan?
The CIBC Aeroplan Visa Infinite earns Aeroplan points — valuable if you fly Air Canada frequently (free checked bags, Aeroplan status-qualifying miles, preferred pricing). The CIBC Aventura earns flexible points redeemable for any airline, making it better for multi-airline travellers. If Air Canada is your primary carrier, choose Aeroplan. If you shop for the cheapest flight across all airlines, Aventura gives more flexibility.
Can I transfer Aventura Points to Aeroplan or other loyalty programs?
No — Aventura Points can only be redeemed through CIBC's Rewards Centre for travel, or for merchandise, gift cards, or statement credits. There are no transfer partners. This is a significant limitation vs. programs like Amex Membership Rewards (which transfer to Aeroplan, BA Avios, etc.).
Do the 4 Priority Pass visits include lounge guests?
The 4 visits are for the primary cardholder only. Additional guests or visits beyond the 4 are charged at $32 USD per person per visit. For families or partners, consider the Scotiabank Passport Visa Infinite (6 visits) or the BMO World Elite Mastercard (4 visits) which may offer better per-visit value.
