How PC Optimum Points Work

PC Optimum is straightforward:

  • 10,000 points = $10 in free groceries or pharmacy items
  • Earn at Loblaws, No Frills, Real Canadian Superstore, Zehrs, Shoppers Drug Mart, Esso/Mobil, Joe Fresh, PC Travel, and more
  • Redeem in 10,000-point increments at checkout — no airline transfer complexity
  • Points stack with digital coupons, bonus events, and your credit card's earn rate

The real power comes from stacking these layers. A properly optimized Saturday Shoppers run can return 25%+ effective value. We'll show you how.

The Stacking Strategy: How to Reach 20%+ Returns

The credit card earn rate is just the foundation. Here's how Canadians who really maximize PC Optimum stack multiple layers:

Layer 1

Your PC Financial Credit Card (Base Rate)

30–40 pts/$1 at Loblaws automatically on every purchase. No action needed.

Layer 2

Weekly In-Store Bonus Events

PC Optimum runs "Spend $X, earn Y bonus points" events weekly. Common example: spend $100 in one transaction, earn 20,000 bonus points ($20 value). These stack on top of your card earn rate — turning a $120 grocery shop into a 20%+ effective return run.

Layer 3

PC Optimum Digital Coupons (App)

The PC Optimum app offers product-specific bonus coupons each week (e.g., "Buy President's Choice chicken, earn 500 bonus points"). Clip before shopping, earn on top of card + bonus events. Takes 5 minutes per week.

Layer 4

Shoppers Drug Mart Saturday 20x Events

Shoppers frequently runs Saturday promotions offering 20x the points (200 pts/$1). Stack that with your PC Insiders card (50 pts/$1) and you're at 250 pts/$1 on Saturdays — effective 25% cashback on non-prescription Shoppers spend.

Smart Redemption: The 10,000-Point Rule

PC Optimum redemptions come in 10,000-point increments only. Here's how to use that to your advantage:

  • Don't let points pile up: Unredeemed points earn no interest. Redeem at multiples of 10,000 whenever you hit a threshold
  • Time redemptions to big shops: Redeem on a $200+ grocery run so the free value doesn't feel wasted
  • Use Shoppers for prescription pickups: Earn 50 pts/$1 (Insiders) on prescriptions, redeem towards household basics
  • Saturday is the best Shoppers day: Earn max points from Saturday 20x events, then redeem the following week

What If You Don't Shop at Loblaws?

The PC Financial lineup is optimized for Loblaws banner stores. If your primary grocery store is:

  • Metro or Sobeys: Amex Cobalt (5x MR = ~7.5% travel value) or Scotia Gold Amex (6x Scene+)
  • Walmart: PC Financial cards don't earn bonus points at Walmart. Use Rogers Red World Elite (2%) or Simplii Cash Back Visa (4% up to $5K/yr)
  • FreshCo, Food Basics: Simplii Financial Cash Back Visa or Rogers Red World Elite
