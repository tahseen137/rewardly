Best Credit Cards for Food Delivery Canada 2026

Ordering Uber Eats, DoorDash, or Skip three times a week adds up fast. The right card earns 5x on every order — that's $10+ back per $200 in deliveries.

The Key: Does Food Delivery Count as "Dining"?

This is the most important question for food delivery rewards. When you order from Uber Eats, DoorDash, or Skip the Dishes, the merchant category code (MCC) on your credit card statement is typically classified as a restaurant/dining transaction — NOT grocery or food retail. This means cards that earn bonus points on dining typically earn those bonus rates on delivery orders too.

This is especially true for Amex Cobalt, which explicitly earns 5x on "food and drink" — a broad category that includes both sit-down restaurants AND delivery apps. Verified by Amex Canada cardholders across all three major delivery platforms in Canada.

UberOne and DoorDash DashPass — Are They Worth It?

Uber One ($9.99/month) and DashPass ($9.99/month) both offer free delivery and discounts on orders, typically paying off with 2–3 orders per month. These subscription fees also earn 5x MR on the Amex Cobalt (streaming/subscription category), making them slightly more valuable for Cobalt holders. If you're ordering 8+ times per month from a single platform, a subscription saves more than its cost.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the Amex Cobalt earn 5x on Uber Eats specifically?
Yes — Uber Eats transactions in Canada typically code as food service / restaurant, which falls under the Cobalt's 5x "food and drink" category. This is confirmed by Amex Canada's terms, which state that the 5x earn applies to eligible purchases at restaurants and food delivery services. Uber Eats Canada is explicitly included. Some Cobalt cardholders also earn 1x on the Uber Cash top-up (wallet loading) — the 5x applies to the food transaction itself, not wallet funding.
What's the best way to pay for Skip the Dishes?
Pay directly with your Amex Cobalt at checkout (not through Skip Credit) for the full 5x earn. Paying through Skip Credits loaded to your account may not earn credit card rewards on the loaded amount. Pay the delivery transaction directly with your physical or digital card for the best rewards.
