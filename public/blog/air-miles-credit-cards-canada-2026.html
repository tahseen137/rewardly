Over 11 million Canadians collect AIR MILES. The right credit card is the fastest way to earn Miles at grocery stores, gas stations, and everywhere you shop. Here's what earns the most.

How AIR MILES Works in Canada

AIR MILES is one of Canada's oldest loyalty programs, operated by LoyaltyOne. Miles are split into two "buckets": AIR MILES Cash (redeemable for instant discounts at sponsor stores — 95 Cash Miles = $10 off) and AIR MILES Dream (saved for bigger rewards: flights, hotels, merchandise, vacation packages).

Miles are earned by scanning your AIR MILES card at sponsor locations (Shell, Rexall, Metro/Food Basics, IGA, FreshCo, Safeway, Sport Chek, Staples, and others) and by spending on an AIR MILES co-branded credit card.

AIR MILES value: Cash Miles ≈ 10.5¢ each ($10 per 95 miles). Dream Miles vary — flights typically yield 8–12¢ per Mile for domestic routes, and potentially more for premium international bookings. Merchandise and gift cards typically give 5–8¢/Mile. Focus on Cash or flights for best value.

AIR MILES Cash vs. Dream — Which Is Better?

Every Mile you earn goes into your account and can be directed to Cash or Dream (you split the allocation in your online account — default is 50/50 on most cards, but you can change it).

AIR MILES Cash: 95 Miles = $10 off at partner stores (Shell, Rexall, Metro, Sport Chek, etc.). Effective rate: ~10.5¢ per Mile. Instant redemption at checkout. No blackout dates.

AIR MILES Dream: Redeemable for flights, vacations, merchandise, gift cards, event tickets, and statement credits. Value varies wildly — 0.4¢/Mile for some merchandise redemptions to 12¢+/Mile for premium flight bookings. The highest-value Dream redemptions are typically short-haul WestJet or Air Canada domestic flights booked during a Miles sale.

General recommendation: Redirect to AIR MILES Cash for predictable, immediate value at 10.5¢/Mile. Use Dream Miles only if you actively monitor AIR MILES Flight sales (often deeply discounted during quarterly promotions). Merchandise and gift card redemptions are typically poor value.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do AIR MILES expire?
AIR MILES Cash Miles expire 5 years after they are earned if there's been no activity on your account. Dream Miles expire if there's been no earning or redeeming activity for 5 consecutive years. Activity resets the clock. The BMO AIR MILES credit card counts as account activity, so holding the card effectively prevents expiry.
Can I transfer AIR MILES to other loyalty programs?
No — AIR MILES cannot be transferred to airline miles or other loyalty currencies. They're a closed program. However, AIR MILES has conversion partnerships with select hotel programs during promotions. For transfer flexibility, Aeroplan (transfers to Air Canada), Amex Membership Rewards (transfers to Aeroplan, BA, Marriott), or RBC Avion (transfers to BA, Cathay Pacific) are better choices.
Is AIR MILES still worth it in 2026?
Yes, with caveats. AIR MILES Cash at 10.5¢/Mile is competitive for grocery and gas spending at Metro and Shell. The program has been devalued relative to its peak (program changes in 2016–2017 significantly reduced value), but Cash Miles redemptions remain solid. If you shop at partner stores regularly and want instant cash-off redemptions, AIR MILES remains worthwhile. If you primarily shop at Loblaws or Sobeys chains, PC Optimum or Scene+ offer higher grocery earn rates.
What's the best AIR MILES welcome bonus?
The BMO AIR MILES World Elite typically offers 2,000–3,000 bonus Miles for spending $3,000 in the first 3 months. At Cash value, that's roughly $210–$315 in Miles — a solid bonus for a $120 annual fee card (waived year 1). Watch for elevated promotions that sometimes offer 5,000 bonus Miles through targeted channels.
