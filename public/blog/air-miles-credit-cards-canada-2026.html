AIR MILES is Canada's original loyalty program — with 10+ million active collectors. The BMO Air Miles World Elite Mastercard is the top co-branded card, but Amex cards also earn Miles. Here's how to maximize both.

Understanding AIR MILES in Canada

AIR MILES Reward Program is operated by LoyaltyOne (a subsidiary of Loyalty Ventures) and has been Canada's second-largest loyalty program for over 30 years. Unlike Aeroplan (which primarily focuses on flights) or PC Optimum (grocery redemptions), AIR MILES offers redemptions across three categories:

Redemption TypeValue Per MileBest For
AIR MILES Dream (Flights)~$0.10–$0.18/mileFlight redemptions — highest value
AIR MILES Cash$0.10/mile (fixed)Instant redemption at 240+ Canadian brands
AIR MILES Shop (Merchandise)~$0.05–$0.08/mileElectronics, gift cards, merchandise — lower value

Key program structure: Each AIR MILES Reward Mile earned is split: 70% goes to your Dream balance (for flights, vacations) and 30% goes to your Cash balance (for in-store cash redemption at $10 per 95 Cash Miles). You can choose to redirect all miles to Dream or Cash, but the default split is 70/30. The Cash Miles value is effectively $0.105/mile ($10 / 95 miles).

How to Maximize AIR MILES

1. Stack in-store loyalty earn + BMO credit card earn. At Shell gas stations: scan your AIR MILES loyalty card for partner miles (2.5 miles/$1 on fuel) AND pay with your BMO Air Miles WE MC for additional card miles. Stack both to maximize your per-dollar return.

2. Use the AIR MILES eStore for online purchases. Shopping through airmiles.ca's online mall earns bonus miles at 200+ retailers — Amazon, Best Buy, Gap, Hotels.com, and more. Rates range from 2x to 15x miles per purchase. Always start shopping through the eStore portal instead of going directly to retailer websites.

3. Redeem Dream Miles for flights under $500 for maximum value. AIR MILES Dream flight redemptions typically deliver the best value ($0.12–0.18/mile) when used for short-to-medium haul flights rather than international business class (where Aeroplan is far superior).

Frequently Asked Questions

Do AIR MILES expire in Canada?
AIR MILES Cash Miles do not expire as long as your account is active. AIR MILES Dream Miles expire after 5 years of account inactivity (no earning or redeeming). With a BMO Air Miles credit card in regular use, expiry is almost never an issue. Check your expiry status at airmiles.ca under "My Account." Note that program rules have changed historically — check current terms at airmiles.ca for the most up-to-date expiry policy.
Can I redeem AIR MILES for any flight or only Air Canada?
Despite the name, AIR MILES are not tied exclusively to Air Canada. AIR MILES Dream Miles can be redeemed for flights on a variety of airlines through the AIR MILES travel portal, including Air Canada, WestJet, and select international carriers. However, the selection and redemption rates vary. For Air Canada specifically and much broader airline access, Aeroplan is a more powerful program for flight redemptions.
