Where the Gold Amex Wins

Grocery and Dining (by a lot): The Gold Amex earns 5x Scene+ on grocery and dining — effectively 5% return (Scene+ = 1¢/pt). The Momentum VI earns 4% on grocery but only 1% on dining. For any household spending significantly at restaurants, the Gold Amex earns 5 percentage points more per dollar on dining. On a combined $1,500/month grocery + dining household, the difference is $900/year (Gold) vs ~$660/year (Momentum, assuming $1,000 grocery + $500 dining) — a significant gap.

0% Foreign Transaction Fee: The Gold Amex charges no FX fee on foreign currency purchases. The Momentum VI charges 2.5%. On a $5,000 international trip, the Gold saves $125. For frequent travelers, this alone can be worth switching cards.

No Grocery Cap: The Gold Amex has no annual cap on the 5x grocery rate (6x at Sobeys-banner stores). The Momentum VI+ caps the 4% at $25,000/year across grocery + recurring bills + streaming combined (gas earns 2%, not 4%). At $1,000/month grocery, the Momentum VI cap is hit before year-end if you're also filling gas and paying bills on the same card.

Where the Momentum VI Wins

Recurring Bills (4% vs 1%): The Momentum VI earns 4% on recurring bill payments — Netflix, Spotify, phone bills, gym memberships, insurance premiums, utilities. The Gold Amex earns only 1% on these. Households with $300–$500/month in recurring bills earn $144–$240 extra per year on those categories with the Momentum.

Gas (2% vs 3%): The Momentum VI+ earns 2% at gas stations; the Gold Amex earns 3x Scene+ (~3%) — Gold Amex wins on gas. Not a massive difference, but Momentum wins this category.

Pure Cash Back Simplicity: The Momentum VI pays annual cash back as a February statement credit — straightforward, no points program to manage, no redemption portal. The Gold Amex requires managing Scene+ points, understanding the value (1¢/pt), and choosing redemption options. For those who want set-and-forget simplicity, Momentum wins.

Universal Visa Acceptance: Visa is accepted at more places than American Express in Canada — Costco, some smaller merchants, and a few retailers accept Visa only. The Amex gap has narrowed significantly (most major Canadian retailers now accept Amex), but in some scenarios Visa has broader coverage.

Which Should You Choose?

Choose Scotia Gold Amex if:

You spend significantly on dining and restaurants

You travel internationally and hate FX fees

You shop primarily at Sobeys/IGA/FreshCo/Safeway (Scene+ partner)

Your monthly recurring bills are under $200

You're comfortable with a points program vs. cash back

Choose Momentum VI if:

You have high recurring bills ($300+/month)

You rarely dine out or mostly cook at home

You want pure cash back — no points program to manage

Costco is your primary grocery/gas destination

You don't travel internationally

The ideal two-card setup: Some households hold both — Scotia Gold Amex for grocery and dining, Momentum VI for recurring bills and gas. Combined annual fee is $240, but the incremental earnings on the second card's categories often justify it. Run the numbers on your actual spending first.

A Third Option Worth Considering

If the dining/restaurant category is your biggest spend, the Amex Cobalt earns 5x MR on dining AND grocery AND streaming — and transferred to Aeroplan at 1:1+10%, that's 5.5 Aeroplan pts per dollar at ~1.5–3¢/pt = 8–16% effective return. The Cobalt costs $191.88/year but earns dramatically more for active Aeroplan collectors.

If you want no-fee and great grocery earn: the BMO eclipse rise earns 5x BMO Rewards (~1.7%) on grocery, dining, gas, drugstores, and streaming with no annual fee.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do both cards have the same travel insurance?
Nearly identical — both offer $2M emergency medical for up to 25 days per trip, trip cancellation at $1,500/person, trip interruption at $2,500/person, and rental CDW. The Momentum VI has a slightly longer rental CDW window (48 days vs. standard). Both require you to charge the travel to the card to activate coverage.
Can Scene+ points from the Gold Amex be redeemed at Sobeys for groceries?
Yes — Scene+ points are redeemable directly at Sobeys, IGA, FreshCo, Safeway, Farm Boy, and other Sobeys-banner stores. 1,000 Scene+ = $10 in free groceries. You can also redeem at Cineplex, for travel through the Scene+ travel portal, or for other experiences. The redemption is straightforward at checkout (scan Scene+ app or card, choose to redeem).
Which card earns more on a $5,000/month household spend?
It depends entirely on how that $5,000 is allocated. If groceries/dining dominate: Gold Amex wins (5% vs 4%). If recurring bills and gas dominate: Momentum VI wins (4% vs 1% on bills). A typical split of $1,500 grocery + $500 dining + $200 gas + $300 bills + $2,500 other might look like: Gold Amex ≈ $225/month | Momentum VI ≈ $150/month. Run your actual numbers.
