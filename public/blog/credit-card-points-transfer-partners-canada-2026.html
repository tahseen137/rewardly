Credit Card Points Transfer Partners Canada 2026

Transferring points to airline miles can double or triple their value. Here's every Canadian loyalty program's transfer options — and which transfers are worth it.

Why Transfers Matter: The Value Multiplier

Most Canadians redeem credit card points for statement credits or merchandise — typically at 1¢ per point or less. The same points transferred to an airline frequent flyer program can be worth 1.5–3¢ each when redeemed for business class flights. This value gap is why points transfer partners are the most powerful feature of flexible rewards currencies.

Transfer partners are airline or hotel loyalty programs that allow you to move points from a credit card program (like Amex Membership Rewards or Avion) into their program at a set ratio. Most Canadian credit card programs offer at least some transfer options, but they vary dramatically in quality and quantity.

Key principle: Flexible points programs (Amex MR, Avion, Chase UR) are more valuable than fixed programs (Air Miles, Scene+) precisely because they transfer to multiple partners. Never surrender flexibility without knowing what you're getting in exchange.

Amex Membership Rewards (Canada) — Transfer Partners

AMEX MR

American Express Membership Rewards

Earned via: Amex Cobalt (5x dining), Amex Gold Rewards (2x travel/grocery), Amex Platinum, Amex Business Gold

Amex MR is Canada's most flexible points currency with the broadest set of transfer partners. The standout transfer is Aeroplan at 1:1 — sending Amex MR to Aeroplan is often the highest-value use of Amex points in Canada, especially for business class redemptions on Air Canada or Star Alliance partners.

Aeroplan — Transfer Partners (Outbound)

AERO PLAN

Aeroplan (Air Canada Loyalty)

Earned via: TD Aeroplan VI, CIBC Aeroplan VI, Amex (via transfer), Amex Cobalt (via transfer)

Aeroplan is both a transfer destination (from Amex MR, Avion) and itself has reciprocal earning/redemption agreements with Star Alliance and select partners. You cannot directly transfer Aeroplan points to other airline programs, but you CAN redeem Aeroplan for flights on all Star Alliance carriers.

RBC Avion Rewards — Transfer Partners

AVION

RBC Avion Rewards

Earned via: RBC Avion Visa Infinite, RBC Avion Visa Infinite Privilege, RBC ION+ Visa

Avion points are one of the better-kept secrets in Canadian loyalty — they transfer to multiple airline programs and have solid redemption options. The flagship transfer is British Airways Avios (1:1), which unlocks short-haul North American flights at very low point costs using Avios distance-based pricing.

Cashback currencies (Rogers Red, Tangerine, CIBC Dividend) have no transfer partners — they're cashback, not points. This is a feature, not a bug. What you lose in transfer optionality you gain in simplicity and guaranteed value. Rogers Red's 1.5–2% is worth exactly 1.5–2¢ of every dollar spent (2% with Rogers/Fido/Shaw service, 1.5% without), no matter what.
