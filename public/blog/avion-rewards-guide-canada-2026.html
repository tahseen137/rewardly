Avion Rewards Guide: Everything About RBC's Points Program

Avion is RBC's flexible travel and lifestyle rewards program. Unlike airline-specific programs, Avion points transfer to multiple airline partners — making them among the most versatile points in Canada.

What Is Avion Rewards?

Avion Rewards is RBC's proprietary travel rewards program, available exclusively through RBC credit cards. Avion points can be redeemed through the RBC travel portal (flights, hotels, car rentals), transferred to airline loyalty programs, used for merchandise, or converted to cash. The key differentiator: Avion transfers to six airline programs, including British Airways Avios and American Airlines AAdvantage — enabling premium cabin redemptions at significantly better value than the RBC travel portal alone.

Key insight: The RBC travel portal redemption rate is about 1¢/point. Airline transfers typically yield 1.5–3¢/point depending on the route and cabin. Transfer to a partner before redeeming — don't just book through the portal unless the fare isn't available elsewhere.

Do Avion Points Expire?

Avion points do not expire as long as your RBC Avion credit card account is open and active. Once you cancel your last Avion-earning card, any remaining points typically expire within 90 days. Transfer pending points to an airline program before closing any RBC Avion card.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I transfer Avion points to Aeroplan?
No — Aeroplan is not an Avion transfer partner. Air Canada and Aeroplan are separate from RBC's Avion program. You can book Air Canada flights through the RBC travel portal at 1¢/pt, but cannot transfer to Aeroplan miles. If you want Aeroplan, the TD Aeroplan Visa Infinite or CIBC Aeroplan Visa Infinite are the primary earning vehicles.
What's the minimum transfer to airline partners?
Minimums vary by partner. British Airways Avios minimum transfer is 5,000 Avion points. American AAdvantage minimum is typically 5,000 points. Always check the current requirements on the Avion Rewards website before initiating a transfer.
How long do Avion transfers take?
Transfer timelines vary by partner: British Airways Avios typically takes 3–5 business days. American AAdvantage transfers usually complete within 1–3 days. Cathay Pacific Asia Miles can take up to 7 business days. Plan ahead — don't initiate a transfer expecting same-day availability for a booking.
Is there an Avion no-fee card?
No dedicated no-fee Avion card exists. The entry-level RBC Rewards+ Visa ($0 fee) earns RBC Rewards points, which can be redeemed for travel through the portal but do NOT transfer to airline partners — those transfers are exclusive to Avion Visa Infinite and Platinum cards.
How many Avion points for a round-trip to Europe?
Through the RBC travel portal: approximately 130,000–180,000 points for a round-trip economy ticket at ~1¢/pt (matching a $1,300–$1,800 cash fare). Through British Airways Avios (transferred 1:1 from Avion): a round-trip to Europe in economy costs about 50,000–60,000 Avios (26,000 each way × 2), with taxes — potentially yielding 2–3¢ per Avion point if the cash fare would've been $1,000+.
