The Simplii Financial Cash Back Visa is the best no-annual-fee dining card in Canada. Four percent cash back at restaurants and bars — on the first $5,000 of dining spend per year — is a rate most paid cards don't match. At $5,000 in dining, you earn $200. The card is free to hold.

Simplii Financial is CIBC's digital banking brand — a direct bank with no monthly fees. The Cash Back Visa is exclusively available to Simplii customers, making it a compelling reason to open a Simplii chequing account if you eat out regularly.

The 1.5% on gas, grocery, pharmacy, and pre-authorized payments is competitive for a no-fee card. The category spread is wider than the Tangerine Money-Back card (2% on only 2–3 chosen categories) — Simplii covers all four major everyday categories at 1.5%, plus the headline 4% dining rate.

⚠️ The $5,000 dining cap: At $5,000 in restaurant spending, your 4% rate earns $200 and then drops to 0.5% for additional dining. If you spend significantly more than $417/month dining out, the cap limits your upside. In that case, consider pairing with a paid dining card like the Amex Cobalt (5× dining, no cap) for spend above $5K.

Cash Back Redemption

Cash back earned with the Simplii card is deposited directly to your Simplii chequing account once per year — on your anniversary date. You cannot redeem earlier, and there is no minimum threshold. The annual deposit makes the cash back feel like a bonus rather than a monthly micro-adjustment.

Unlike some cash back cards that require you to log in and initiate a redemption, Simplii auto-deposits — you don't need to do anything to collect.

The Simplii card occupies a unique niche: 4% dining at no annual fee. The Amex Cobalt and Scotia Gold Amex both offer higher dining rates (5–6%), but carry annual fees. For diners who spend less than $3,000/yr at restaurants, the Simplii card's zero fee makes it more profitable than a paid card. Above $3,000+/yr, the paid card starts winning.

Who Should Get the Simplii Cash Back Visa

This card earns its place in the wallet for anyone who:

  • Eats out regularly but doesn't want to pay an annual fee for the privilege
  • Wants straightforward cash back with no points complexity
  • Already banks with Simplii Financial (or is willing to open an account for this card)
  • Wants a reliable 1.5% card on gas, groceries, and pharmacy as a secondary card
  • Is building their credit history with a no-fee product

It is less ideal for frequent travelers who need trip cancellation and emergency medical coverage, heavy diners spending over $5,000/yr at restaurants, or people who prefer points over cash back.

Getting the Card: Simplii Financial Account Required

To apply for the Simplii Cash Back Visa, you need to be (or become) a Simplii Financial customer. Simplii is a digital-only bank owned by CIBC — there are no branches, but it integrates fully with the Interac e-Transfer network, has a mobile app, and offers fee-free chequing with no monthly fee requirements. Opening a Simplii account takes about 10 minutes online.

Rewardly Verdict — 4.0 / 5

For diners who don't want to pay an annual fee, the Simplii Financial Cash Back Visa is the best dining card in Canada. Four percent back at restaurants with zero cost to hold is a compelling combination — most cards that offer 4%+ dining charge $120–$155/yr.

The weaknesses are real: no travel insurance makes it a poor choice as a sole travel card, and the $5,000 dining cap limits high-spending diners. But as a companion card in a two-card wallet — Simplii for dining, Rogers Red (or another card) for everything else — it maximizes returns without adding annual fee cost. Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to bank with Simplii to get this card?

Yes. The Simplii Cash Back Visa is only available to Simplii Financial customers. You'll need to open a free Simplii chequing account to apply. There's no monthly fee and no minimum balance requirement for the basic account.

Does DoorDash and Uber Eats earn 4%?

Yes — food delivery services are included in the dining category. Uber Eats, DoorDash, and SkipTheDishes charges all earn 4% up to the $5,000 annual cap.

When is cash back paid out?

Cash back is deposited once per year to your Simplii chequing account, on your credit card anniversary date. You don't need to log in or initiate any action — it's automatic.

Is there a welcome bonus?

Simplii runs limited-time promotions offering additional cash back on spending in the first few months. Check the Simplii website at the time of application for current offers — these can substantially increase first-year value.

What is the credit limit?

Credit limits are assigned individually based on credit history and income at the time of application. There is no published minimum or maximum.
