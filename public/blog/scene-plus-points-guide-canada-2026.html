Scene+ Points Guide Canada 2026

Everything you need to know: how to earn Scene+ at Scotiabank, Cineplex, Empire stores, and how to redeem for maximum value.

📖 11 min read

Scene+ is one of Canada's largest loyalty programs — a partnership between Scotiabank and Cineplex that merged in 2021 to create a combined ecosystem covering banking, movies, grocery, dining, and travel. If you bank with Scotiabank or hold one of its credit cards, you're likely earning Scene+ points already. This guide explains how to earn them efficiently and how to get the most value when you redeem.

What Is Scene+?

Scene+ launched as the merged program combining Scotiabank's banking rewards and Cineplex's movie loyalty program (formerly just "Scene"). Today, Scene+ is more than just movies — it's a broad everyday loyalty program with partners in:

  • Entertainment — Cineplex movies, The Rec Room, Playdium
  • Grocery — Empire Company stores (Sobeys, IGA, Safeway, FreshCo, Foodland, Farm Boy, Needs)
  • Dining — Harvey's, Swiss Chalet, Montana's, Kelsey's, East Side Mario's, and more
  • Banking & Credit Cards — Scotiabank credit cards and debit transactions
  • Travel — Scene+ Travel portal (flights, hotels, car rentals)
  • Retail — Select Scene+ partners

Points earned across all these channels are pooled in one account under your Scene+ membership, accessible via the Scene+ app or website.

How Much Is a Scene+ Point Worth?

1 Scene+ point = $0.01 (1 cent) when redeemed at face value for movies, groceries, or Scene+ Travel bookings. This is a simple, transparent value — 100 points = $1. No complex airline-style redemption charts or partner transfer ratios to navigate.

Unlike Aeroplan (where point value swings from 0.5¢ to 2¢+ depending on redemption), Scene+ maintains a consistent 1¢ per point across its primary redemption channels. What you see is what you get.

Non-Card Ways to Earn Scene+

Empire Grocery Stores

You earn Scene+ on purchases at Empire Company's grocery banners even without a Scotiabank credit card. Simply scan your Scene+ app or membership card at checkout at Sobeys, IGA, Safeway, FreshCo, Foodland, Farm Boy, or Needs. Base earn is typically 1 point per dollar, with bonus multipliers on selected items and weekly offers in the Scene+ app.

Cineplex

Scene+ originated from the Cineplex Scene movie loyalty program. You earn points on every movie ticket, food purchase, and gaming session at Cineplex locations, The Rec Room, and Playdium. Standard movie ticket: approximately 100–200 points. Bonus multipliers apply for Scene+ member screenings, IMAX, D-BOX, and VIP experiences.

Harvey's and Swiss Chalet

Scan your Scene+ membership at Harvey's or Swiss Chalet to earn approximately 1 point per dollar on food purchases. Regular bonus offers through the Scene+ app add occasional 3–5× multiplier events at these restaurant partners.

Scotiabank Banking

Scotiabank checking accounts and eligible debit card transactions earn Scene+ points. The earn rate varies by account tier. Scotiabank Ultimate Package and Preferred Package chequing accounts include monthly Scene+ bonus points.

Best Ways to Redeem Scene+ Points

Movies and Entertainment at Cineplex (1¢/pt)

The original redemption: use Scene+ points for Cineplex movie tickets, food, gaming, and experiences. Redemption is simple — select "Pay with Scene+" in the Cineplex app or at the box office. Value is consistently 1¢ per point.

Grocery Redemption at Empire Stores (1¢/pt)

Redeem directly at the checkout at Sobeys, IGA, FreshCo, or other Empire stores. Tell the cashier you want to use Scene+ points, and the amount is deducted from your bill in real time. No minimum redemption. Value is 1¢ per point.

Scene+ Travel Portal (1¢/pt)

Book flights, hotels, car rentals, and vacation packages through the Scene+ Travel portal and redeem points at checkout. Points apply at 1¢ each, and you can mix points and payment freely. There are no blackout dates. This is comparable in value to grocery/movie redemptions but offers more flexibility in destinations.

Statement Credits on Eligible Scotiabank Cards (1¢/pt)

Some Scotiabank cards allow statement credit redemption at 1¢ per point. Check your specific card's terms — not all cards support this. Where available, this is as flexible as cash back.

What to Avoid When Redeeming Scene+

Not all redemption options give you 1¢ per point. Avoid:

  • Gift cards from select partners — often 0.8¢ per point or less
  • Merchandise from the Scene+ catalog — typically 0.5–0.8¢ per point
  • Transfers to other programs — Scene+ does not transfer to airline programs, so there is no transfer path to Aeroplan or similar
Best redemption ranking (by value):
1. Grocery at Empire stores — 1¢/pt, instant at checkout
2. Cineplex movies/entertainment — 1¢/pt
3. Scene+ Travel portal — 1¢/pt, flexible destinations
4. Statement credit (where available) — 1¢/pt
5. Gift cards, merchandise — 0.5–0.8¢/pt (avoid unless necessary)

Maximizing Scene+ Points in 2026 — Top Strategies

Strategy 1: Use the Scotia Gold Amex for All Grocery and Dining

At 6 pts/$ on Empire stores, dining, and food delivery, the Scotia Gold Amex earns 6% effectively at grocery and restaurants. On $600/month of combined grocery and dining, that's 43,200 points per year — worth $432 in free groceries or movies.

Strategy 2: Stack Card Points with In-Store Earn

When shopping at Sobeys with your Scotia Gold Amex, you earn both the card's 6 pts/$ on grocery AND the in-store Scene+ earn on your membership card. These stack. Always scan your Scene+ app even when paying with a Scotiabank card to capture both streams.

Strategy 3: Use Scene+ App Offers and Bonus Events

The Scene+ app regularly features bonus point offers: 3× on specific items at Empire stores, extra points at Cineplex for specific showtimes, restaurant event promotions. Check the app weekly and activate offers before shopping to capture multipliers.

Strategy 4: Welcome Bonuses on New Cards

Scotiabank regularly offers 20,000–45,000 point welcome bonuses on Scene+ credit cards — worth $200–$450 — after meeting a spending threshold in the first three months. These alone can fund several months of grocery or entertainment.

Strategy 5: Concentrate Empire Grocery Spend

If you currently split grocery shopping across Loblaws, No Frills, and Sobeys, consider shifting more to Sobeys/IGA/FreshCo to concentrate your earn in the Scene+ ecosystem. At 6 pts/$ (with the Gold Amex) plus in-store earn, Empire stores become your highest-return grocery option.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do Scene+ points expire?

Scene+ points do not expire as long as your account remains active (at least one transaction every 18 months). If your account goes inactive for 18+ months with no earn or redeem activity, points may expire. Keep the account active with any small transaction — a Cineplex scan or Empire store purchase resets the clock.

Can I transfer Scene+ points to Aeroplan?

No — Scene+ does not have a transfer partnership with Aeroplan or any airline loyalty program. Scene+ is a self-contained ecosystem. If airline miles are your goal, you need a card that earns directly in the Aeroplan program (TD Aeroplan, CIBC Aeroplan, or Amex to Aeroplan via Membership Rewards).

How do I check my Scene+ balance?

Log in to the Scene+ app (iOS/Android) or scene.ca. Your balance shows all points earned from credit card spending, in-store scans, and bonus offers. The app also shows pending offers to activate.

Can I combine Scene+ accounts with a family member?

Scene+ does not currently support household account pooling in the same way some programs (like Aeroplan) do. Each member has their own balance. Some redemptions (like booking a movie for your family) use one person's points for the entire group's tickets.

Is the Scotia Gold Amex the best Scene+ card?

For most Canadians: yes. Six points per dollar on grocery, dining, food delivery, and entertainment is the highest Scene+ earn rate available on a personal credit card. The $120/yr fee (waived first year) pays for itself quickly. If you don't need Amex acceptance (some merchants don't take it), the Scotiabank Passport Visa Infinite offers 3 pts/$ on similar categories with no foreign transaction fee and lounge access.
