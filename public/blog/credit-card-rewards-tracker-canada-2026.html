What Is a Credit Card Rewards Tracker?

A credit card rewards tracker is an app that helps you earn more points, miles, and cashback from your existing credit cards by:

  • Store-specific recommendations: Telling you which card earns the most at a specific merchant
  • Category tracking: Knowing which card offers bonus multipliers on groceries, dining, gas, travel, etc.
  • Bonus alerts: Notifying you when a card's category bonuses change or promotional multipliers are active
  • Portfolio analysis: Identifying gaps in your card wallet where you're not earning at maximum rates

Without a tracker, most multi-card Canadians default to one card for everything ‚Äî missing 2‚Äì5% in bonus category earn every single day. Over a year, that's $400‚Äì1,500 in uncaptured rewards for a typical Canadian household.
