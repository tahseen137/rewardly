Frequently Asked Questions

When is cash back paid out on the Scotia Momentum VI?
Once per year in November as a statement credit. This is the card's biggest drawback vs. competitors — you earn cash back all year but only receive it in November. There's no way to request early redemption. If you need more frequent access to your rewards, consider CIBC Dividend VI (quarterly, on demand after $25 minimum) or Tangerine (monthly, automatic). The annual payout means you're effectively giving Scotia a float on your earned cash back for up to 12 months.
Does Costco count as a grocery store on the Scotia Momentum VI?
No — Costco warehouses code as MCC 5300 (Warehouse Clubs), not 5411 (Grocery Stores). Scotia Momentum VI earns only 1% at Costco warehouse. Additionally, Costco Canada only accepts Mastercard payments in-store (not Visa). The Scotia Momentum VI is a Visa card and cannot be used inside Costco stores, though it works for Costco.ca online orders.
Which is better — Scotia Momentum VI or Scotia Gold Amex?
Different purposes: Scotia Momentum VI+ = pure cash back at 4% on grocery/bills/streaming (2% on gas). Scotia Gold Amex = Scene+ travel points (6x dining, 5x grocery/gas/transit, 3x everywhere else) with 0% FX. If you want cash, Scotia Momentum. If you want travel points and international shopping, Scotia Gold Amex. Many Scotiabank customers hold both cards — using Momentum for recurring bills and grocery (4% cash back) and gas (2% cash back), and Gold Amex for dining, US purchases, and travel bookings (Scene+ points + 0% FX).

Rewardly Verdict

The Scotiabank Momentum Visa Infinite is one of the best pure cash-back cards in Canada — the 4% on groceries, streaming, AND recurring bills — with 2% on gas and transit — is among the broadest bonus coverage at this fee tier in Canada. The $120 fee is easily justified by typical Canadian family spending ($554 net return estimate). The annual cash-back payout timing is the main frustration. For Scotiabank customers, pairing the Momentum VI (cash back on everyday spend) with the Scotia Gold Amex (Scene+ points + 0% FX on international purchases) creates one of the best 2-card setups available.
