The American Express Platinum Card is Canada's most prestigious consumer card. At $799/year, it's expensive — but the combination of Centurion Lounge access, Fine Hotels & Resorts (free breakfast for two, room upgrades, $100 property credit), automatic Marriott Gold and Hilton Gold status, and the $200 annual travel credit delivers far more than $799 in value for frequent travellers. The question isn't whether the benefits exceed the fee — they do, easily. The question is whether you'll use them.

The Core Benefits That Justify $799

$400–$800/yr

☕ Centurion Lounge Access (Unlimited)

Access to American Express Centurion Lounges worldwide — widely considered the best airport lounges available to any cardholder. In Canada: Toronto Pearson (T1), Vancouver YVR. US: NYC JFK, LAX, LAS, DFW, MIA, PHX, SEA, CLT, SFO, MCO, DEN, PHL, ATL, BOS, CLT, IAH, ORD. Premium food, open bar, spa services. Add up to 2 guests free. Value: $150–$200+/visit × several visits/year.

$300–$500/stay

🏨 Fine Hotels + Resorts (FH+R)

Book 1,900+ luxury properties through Amex Travel's FH+R program and receive: guaranteed room upgrade (on arrival, if available), early check-in at noon, late checkout at 4pm, daily breakfast for two, $100+ USD property credit (unique to each property), and room-tier upgrades at check-in. On a 2-night stay, these perks are easily worth $300–$500 in tangible value (breakfast alone = $40–$70/person × 2 × 2 days).

$200/yr

✈️ Annual Travel Credit

$200 annual statement credit applied against eligible travel purchases — flights, hotels, car rentals, cruises — charged to your card. Applied automatically. This directly reduces your effective annual fee from $799 to $599.

~$200/yr

🥇 Marriott Bonvoy Gold Elite Status (Auto)

Automatic Marriott Bonvoy Gold Elite status every year — without staying a single night at a Marriott property. Gold gives you: 25% bonus points on stays, enhanced room upgrades, 2pm late checkout, and welcome gifts (typically points). Worth $100–$200+ to frequent Marriott guests. Normally requires 25 qualifying nights/year — the Platinum card grants it with zero stays.

~$200/yr

🟡 Hilton Honors Gold Status (Auto)

Automatic Hilton Honors Gold status every year. Hilton Gold is highly valuable: complimentary breakfast for you and a guest at most full-service Hilton properties (worth $40–$80/morning for two). Also includes space-available room upgrades and 80% bonus points on stays. Breakfast alone: $40 × 2 people × 10 hotel nights = $800/year for a business traveller.

~$100

🌍 Global Entry / NEXUS Credit

Statement credit for the application fee for Global Entry ($100 USD) or NEXUS ($50 CAD) every 4 years. Global Entry gives you PreCheck in US airports, expedited US customs/immigration on entry, and NEXUS border crossings — worth far more than $100 in saved time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Amex Platinum worth it for someone who flies once or twice a year?
Probably not. The card's value is heavily concentrated in lounge access and FH+R hotel benefits — both require active travel to extract. For occasional travellers, the Amex Gold Rewards ($250/year, with $100 travel credit) or Scotiabank Passport Visa Infinite ($150/year, 6 Priority Pass visits) offer much better value-to-fee ratios.
How do I access Centurion Lounges in Canada?
Present your Amex Platinum Card and boarding pass at the Centurion Lounge entrance. In Canada, Centurion Lounges are at Toronto Pearson International (T1) and Vancouver International. You can bring up to 2 guests at no charge. Same-day boarding pass for any airline is required.
Does Amex Platinum have a foreign transaction fee?
Yes — 2.5% foreign transaction fee, which is surprisingly high for a $799 card. For international purchases, use a no-FX card alongside your Platinum (Scotiabank Passport VI or Scotia Gold Amex both waive FX fees). Keep the Platinum for lounge access and hotel benefits, use the no-FX card for spending abroad.
Can I get Amex Platinum if I already have the Amex Cobalt?
Yes — American Express Canada allows you to hold multiple Amex cards simultaneously. Many cardholders hold both Cobalt (5× dining/grocery) and Platinum (lounges, hotel status, FH+R) and use each for its strengths. The welcome bonus on Platinum is only available once per lifetime, so time your application when it's at a maximum offer.
