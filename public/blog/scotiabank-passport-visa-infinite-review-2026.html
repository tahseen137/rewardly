The No-FX-Fee Advantage — This Is the Card's True Superpower

Most Canadian credit cards charge a 2.5% foreign transaction fee on every purchase made in a foreign currency (including online purchases charged in USD). Over the course of a 2-week international trip or consistent US online shopping, this adds up fast:

  • $2,000 USD spend on a trip → $50 in FX fees avoided
  • $5,000 USD/year on Amazon.com, US subscriptions, etc. → $125 saved
  • $10,000 USD/year in international purchases → $250 saved

Pairing zero FX fees with 1 Scene+ pt per dollar on international purchases means you're earning a small reward on top of the fee you're not paying. Compare this to the Scotiabank Momentum Visa Infinite (4% on groceries, but 2.5% FX fee on international — the fee erases all rewards on foreign currency spend).

Key insight: If you spend $5,000+ in USD or other foreign currencies per year, the Passport's zero FX fee alone delivers $125+ in annual savings — covering 83% of the $150 annual fee before earning a single point.

Priority Pass Lounge Access — 6 Free Visits

The Scotiabank Passport comes with a complimentary Priority Pass membership plus 6 free visits per year. Priority Pass is the world's largest independent airport lounge program — access to 1,400+ lounges in 148 countries.

In Canada, Priority Pass covers:

  • Toronto Pearson (YYZ): Maple Leaf Lounge (Air Canada flights only), Plaza Premium Lounge (any flight)
  • Vancouver (YVR): Plaza Premium Lounge
  • Montreal (YUL): Air France Lounge, Plaza Premium
  • Calgary (YYC): Plaza Premium
  • Ottawa (YOW): Aspire Lounge

At $40–$65 CAD per lounge visit, 6 free visits = $240–$390 in value — exceeding the $150 annual fee before any other benefit. For travelers who fly 3+ times a year and value a quiet space before flights, this alone makes the card worth carrying.

After your 6 free visits, additional visits cost ~$32 USD per person through your Priority Pass membership (vs. $65–$100 paying at the door). Guest fees may apply if you bring someone with you.

Scene+ Points Value

Scene+ points are flexible but not maximally valuable for points purists:

  • Cineplex movies: 1,000 pts = $7.99 off (~0.8¢/pt) — best value
  • Scene+ travel portal: 100 pts = $1 (1.0¢/pt)
  • Sobeys/IGA grocery discount: 750 pts = $5 (~0.67¢/pt)
  • Dining statement credits: ~0.7¢/pt
  • Gift cards: ~0.5–0.7¢/pt (varies by retailer)

For travel redemptions at 1¢/pt, the 3× grocery/dining earn = effectively 3% return on those categories. That's competitive with the Scotiabank Momentum at 4% cashback, though Momentum is purely a cashback card (no lounge access, no zero FX).

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Scotiabank Passport the only Canadian card with no FX fees?

No, but it's the best one with both no FX fees and significant additional perks. Other no-FX options include the Scotiabank Gold American Express (3× on dining/groceries but Amex acceptance is more limited), Home Trust Preferred Visa (no FX, no fee, but 1% cashback only), and Brim Financial Mastercard (no FX, up to 1% cashback). The Passport stands out by pairing no-FX with lounge access, strong earn rates, and travel insurance.

Does the zero FX fee apply to online purchases in USD?

Yes. Any purchase in a foreign currency — whether at a physical store abroad or on a US website charging in USD — benefits from the zero FX fee. Amazon.com, US software subscriptions, US Etsy sellers, US online retailers — all avoid the 2.5% fee that other Canadian cards charge.

How do the 6 Priority Pass visits work?

Scotiabank provides a complimentary Priority Pass membership and credits 6 free visits annually to your account. When you arrive at a participating lounge, show your Priority Pass card (digital or physical) — no charge for up to 6 visits per year. After 6 visits, you're billed ~$32 USD per visit. Guest visits cost extra and count toward your 6.

Can I earn Scene+ at Sobeys with my Passport card?

Yes — the Passport earns 3 Scene+ pts per $1 at Sobeys/IGA/Safeway AND you can scan your Scene+ collector card/app at the same Sobeys checkout to earn Scene+ shopping rewards simultaneously. The credit card earn and the store loyalty earn stack.

How does the welcome bonus work?

Typically 25,000 Scene+ pts after your first purchase + additional 5,000 after meeting a minimum spend (around $1,000 in the first 3 months). Check Scotia's current offer — it's been elevated to 40,000–50,000 pts during promotions. At 0.7¢/pt average value, 30,000 pts = ~$210 in value.
