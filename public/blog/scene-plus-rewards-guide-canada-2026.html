Scene+ Rewards Guide Canada 2026

Scene+ is Scotiabank's rewards currency — earn points at Cineplex, Sobeys, IGA, Safeway, and with Scotiabank credit cards. Here's how to earn fast and redeem for maximum value.

What Is Scene+?

Scene+ is a loyalty program jointly operated by Scotiabank and Cineplex. Members earn Scene+ points at Scotiabank credit cards, Cineplex/Empire theatres, Sobeys banner grocery stores (Sobeys, IGA, Safeway, FreshCo, Thrifty Foods), and partner retailers. Points are worth 1¢ each — simple, fixed, no transfer complexity. 10,000 Scene+ points = $10 in redemption value.

Scene+ vs. Aeroplan: Scene+ points are worth a fixed 1¢ each. Aeroplan points can be worth 0.5¢–6¢+ depending on redemption. For simplicity and predictability, Scene+ wins. For maximizing travel value, Aeroplan wins. Many Scotiabank customers use Scene+ for everyday redemptions (groceries, movies) and Aeroplan for travel.

🎬 Cineplex / Empire Theatres

Earn 100 Scene+ points per $1 at Cineplex concessions and ticket purchases (100 pts = 10¢, or effectively 10% back toward future Cineplex purchases). Scene+ card purchases at Cineplex stack: card earn + in-theatre earn. Cineplex also runs bonus point promotions throughout the year.

🛒 Sobeys / IGA / Safeway / FreshCo / Thrifty Foods

Earn Scene+ points on every grocery purchase at Sobeys-banner stores. Base earn: 1 Scene+ point per $1 (worth 0.1¢, or 0.1% on top of card earn). Bonus multipliers: 10x–20x on select products promoted in-store weekly. Scan your Scene+ card (or app) at checkout on top of your Scotiabank credit card to double-dip: card points + Scene+ loyalty points.

🏨 Hotels via Scene+ Travel

Book hotels through Scene+ Travel portal and earn 5–10 Scene+ points per $1 spent, redeemable as statement credits. Competitive with major hotel booking sites in Canada.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do Scene+ points expire?
Scene+ points expire after 12 months of account inactivity — meaning if you don't earn or redeem any points for 12 consecutive months, your points balance is forfeited. Simply making one purchase or redemption resets the 12-month clock. Active Scotia credit cardholders who use their card regularly will never expire their points. Register your Scene+ card online to track your balance and expiry status.
Can I use Scene+ points for cash back or statement credit?
Not as a direct cash back to your bank account. Scene+ points can be applied as statement credits on your Scotiabank credit card against travel purchases made through Scene+ Travel, or against eligible dining, grocery, and entertainment purchases when using your Scotia card. This is effectively a statement credit — reducing your credit card balance — but it's restricted to eligible purchase categories rather than arbitrary statement credit.
How does Scene+ compare to PC Optimum for grocery shoppers?
Very similar in redemption value (both ~1¢/point), but they work at competing grocery chains. Scene+ works at Sobeys, Safeway, IGA, FreshCo, and Thrifty Foods — with Scotiabank credit cards as the best earner. PC Optimum works at Loblaws, No Frills, Real Canadian Superstore, and Shoppers Drug Mart — with PC Financial Mastercard as the best earner. Choose based on where you already shop for groceries. Families near both chains sometimes hold both loyalty programs — using Scene+ at Sobeys and PC Optimum at Loblaws.
