What Is the National Bank World Elite Mastercard?

The National Bank World Elite Mastercard is the flagship consumer rewards card from National Bank of Canada — the country's sixth-largest bank, headquartered in Montreal and dominant in Quebec but available nationally. It earns À La Carte Rewards points: 5 points per $1 on travel and dining, 2 points per $1 on grocery, gas, and pharmacy, and 1 point per $1 everywhere else.

Points are worth approximately 1¢ each for travel redemptions through National Bank's À La Carte Travel platform. The card's most distinctive feature is its additional driver rental car coverage — the only major Canadian card that protects authorized drivers beyond the primary renter — alongside the 60-day emergency medical window it shares with the Desjardins Odyssey.

The Unique Additional-Driver Rental Car Coverage

🚗 What Makes This CDW Coverage Unique in Canada

The National Bank World Elite MC covers all authorized additional drivers listed in the rental agreement, not just the primary cardholder. On virtually every other Canadian card, CDW protects only the main renter. If you and your spouse share driving on a rental, the NBC card covers both — potentially saving $120–200 in additional driver fees on a 7–10 day trip.

Coverage: vehicles with MSRP up to $85,000, for up to 48 days. Includes loss-of-use coverage. Covers both the cardholder and any additional authorized drivers named in the rental contract.

Verdict

4.3 / 5

The National Bank World Elite Mastercard earns its 4.3 with two genuinely unique features: the 60-day emergency medical window (one of Canada's longest) and additional driver CDW coverage (essentially unique in the Canadian market). For couples who rent cars frequently or travellers taking extended trips, these features alone justify the $150 annual fee.

The main reason to choose the Desjardins Odyssey instead: unlimited lounge access at $20 less per year. The main reason to choose the Scotia Gold Amex instead: 0% FX fee and Scene+ integration. But if you want the best rental car protection in Canada plus outstanding travel insurance at a reasonable fee, the NBC World Elite is unmatched.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get the National Bank World Elite MC if I don't live in Quebec? Yes — National Bank serves customers across Canada. You can apply online. Branch availability outside Quebec is more limited than the Big 5, but all banking can be done digitally.
Can I transfer À La Carte Rewards to Aeroplan or airlines? No. À La Carte Rewards do not transfer to airline loyalty programs. All travel redemptions go through National Bank's À La Carte Travel platform or as statement credits against travel charges at approximately 1¢ per point.
Why does additional driver CDW coverage matter? Standard rental car CDW on most credit cards only protects the primary renter (the cardholder). If your spouse or travel companion also drives, they're not covered by most cards. The NBC World Elite explicitly covers additional drivers listed in the rental contract — saving significant daily fees at the car rental counter.
Do unused lounge visits roll over? No — 6 complimentary visits reset each calendar year. Additional visits can be purchased at approximately $32–40 CAD per visit.
What is the interest rate on the National Bank World Elite MC? 19.99% on purchases; 22.99% on cash advances. Standard rates — pay in full each month to avoid interest wiping out all rewards earned.
