PC Optimum is the combined loyalty program of Loblaw Companies Ltd. and Shoppers Drug Mart, launched in 2018. Points are earned by scanning the app (or card) at participating stores and by using the PC Financial Mastercard. 10,000 points = $10 in free groceries or pharmacy purchases — always a fixed 1¢ per 10 points, no devaluations or variable redemption tables.

Maximizing PC Optimum — Proven Strategies

1. Shoppers Drug Mart Gift Card Strategy

Shoppers Drug Mart sells gift cards for hundreds of major brands — Amazon, Indigo, Cineplex, restaurants, and more. During Shoppers' bonus events (20X Fridays, app-exclusive bonus offers), you can earn 20X points on gift card purchases up to a spending cap. Buying gift cards you would have used anyway at 20X = 20% return in PC Optimum points.

Example: On a 20X Shoppers Friday with $200 in gift cards: 200 × 20 = 4,000 points = $4 in future rewards. Pay with PC Financial WE MC for additional 45 pts/$10 = total $4 + $0.90 = $4.90 back on $200 spent. That's 2.45% just in rewards, with the gift card's face value going to a store you'd shop anyway.

2. Stack App Offers with In-Store Bonuses

The PC Optimum app offers personalized bonus point offers each week — often 500–5,000 bonus points for buying specific products. These stack with store-wide bonus events AND your credit card earn. Always check the app before shopping and add active offers. A single shop with 5 product-specific offers can yield 20,000+ points.

3. Prescription Optimization

Shoppers regularly offers bonus points for filling prescriptions — sometimes 10,000 pts (=$10) for new prescriptions or medication transfers. If you're transferring from another pharmacy, Shoppers' transfer bonuses can yield 20,000–50,000 pts. Refill bonuses for ongoing medications also run periodically.

4. PC Insiders Program (formerly PC Plus)

The PC Insiders subscription ($9.99/month) multiplies earn rates on select items and gives exclusive offers. For heavy shoppers, the added points often exceed the subscription cost — but do the math on your spending before subscribing.

How to Redeem PC Optimum Points

PC Optimum points can be redeemed in increments of 10,000 (= $10) at checkout. There is no minimum redemption — you can use 10,000 points for $10 off as many times as you have them.

Redemption locations: All Loblaws-banner stores, Shoppers Drug Mart, Pharmaprix, PC Travel, Joe Fresh online, and Esso/Mobil (at select stations for fuel). Points cannot be transferred to other people or programs. They cannot be used for cash, only store purchases.

Redemption tip: Redeem at Shoppers Drug Mart rather than grocery stores when possible. Shoppers sells a wider range of products (gift cards, cosmetics, electronics) and the redemption goes further in absolute dollar value. Shoppers also has no minimum transaction size for redemption.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do PC Optimum points expire?
Yes — PC Optimum points expire if there's been no earn or redeem activity on your account for 12 consecutive months. Using your PC Financial Mastercard anywhere counts as activity. If you don't have the card, making any purchase at a Loblaws or Shoppers and scanning your app resets the clock.
Can I combine PC Optimum points with a spouse or family member?
Not directly — each account is individual and points cannot be transferred. However, multiple family members can all shop under one account by sharing the app/card scan at checkout, or multiple PC Financial cards in the same household each earn toward the same store loyalty scan. Check with the program for household account options.
Can I use PC Optimum points at self-checkout?
Yes — all Loblaw-banner self-checkout kiosks accept the PC Optimum app QR code or card swipe, and allow point redemption at checkout. You must scan your code before finalizing payment.
Does the PC Financial Mastercard work at stores other than Loblaws/SDM?
Yes — it earns 30 pts per $10 (3% effective) everywhere Mastercard is accepted. But the card's value proposition is strongest at Loblaws-banner and Shoppers locations (4.5%). Using it as your primary card everywhere gives a solid 3% floor on non-partner spend.
What's the fastest way to accumulate a large PC Optimum balance?
Combining: Shoppers Drug Mart gift card purchases during 20X bonus events, using the app's personalized product bonus offers every week, filling prescriptions at Shoppers during transfer bonuses, and always scanning + paying with PC Financial WE MC. Power users report earning $50–$100/month in free groceries from deliberate stacking.
