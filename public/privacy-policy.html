Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

TL;DR: Rewardly collects only what is necessary to deliver its service. Your financial data (card numbers, bank credentials, account balances) is never collected. Location is processed on-device for the AutoPilot feature. AI chat history and card portfolio data sync to our servers so you can access them across your devices. Payment processing is handled by Stripe — we never see or store your card number. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

1. Scope of This Policy

This policy applies to all Rewardly products operated by Motu Inc.:

  • Mobile Rewardly iOS & Android app — distributed through the Apple App Store and Google Play Store
  • Web Rewardly web app — available at rewardly.ca
  • Extension Rewardly Chrome extension — available on the Chrome Web Store

Where data practices differ between platforms, each item is labelled with the relevant tag above.

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Account and Identity

Mobile Web

When you create a Rewardly account, we collect:

  • Email address — used for authentication, transactional emails (password reset, subscription receipts), and account communications.
  • Password — stored as a bcrypt hash by Supabase Auth. We never see your plaintext password.
  • Display name and avatar URL — optional profile fields you can set in Settings.
  • Preferred language — English or French, set during onboarding.
  • Country — Canada or United States, used to filter the card database to your region.
  • Onboarding completion status — a boolean flag so we know whether to show the onboarding flow.

Guest mode is available on the web app. Guest sessions are stored locally in your browser and are not synced to our servers unless you create an account.

2.2 Card Portfolio

Mobile Web

When you add credit cards to your wallet, we store:

  • Card identifiers (e.g. amex-cobalt, rbc-avion-visa-infinite) — internal keys that reference our card database. We never ask for your card number, CVV, expiry date, or cardholder name.
  • Optional nickname and notes — personal labels you attach to a card.
  • Date added — timestamp of when you added the card to your wallet.

This data is synced to Supabase so your wallet is available across your devices.

2.3 Spending Profile and Transaction Data

Mobile Web

To power the recommendation engine and spending insights, we collect:

  • Spending profile — estimated monthly spend by category (groceries, dining, travel, gas, etc.) that you enter manually.
  • Statement uploads — if you upload a CSV bank statement, we store the parsed transaction records (merchant name, amount, date, inferred category) in our database. We support CSV exports from 8 Canadian banks. Raw CSV files are processed server-side and then discarded; only the structured transaction records are retained.
  • Merchant category mappings — any custom merchant-to-category assignments you create are saved to your account.

We never ask for and never receive your online banking login credentials. Statement import is CSV-only, not a live bank connection.

2.4 AI Chat History (Sage)

Mobile Web

Sage is Rewardly's AI assistant, powered by Anthropic Claude or OpenAI GPT (depending on availability). When you use Sage:

  • Conversation messages — your messages and Sage's replies are stored in our database, associated with your user account, so you can access your conversation history across sessions.
  • Conversation metadata — title (auto-generated from the first message), timestamps, and the AI model used.
  • Token usage — aggregate token counts per billing period, used to enforce usage limits on free-tier accounts.
  • Card and portfolio context — your wallet and spending profile are sent to the AI provider as context for each message, so Sage can give personalized recommendations. This context is not stored separately; it is assembled from your existing profile data at request time.

Your messages are transmitted to a third-party AI provider (Anthropic or OpenAI) to generate responses. See Section 5 for details on their data handling.

2.5 Location Data (AutoPilot)

Mobile

AutoPilot is an opt-in feature that sends you a push notification recommending the best card in your wallet when you arrive near a pinned merchant location. Location handling is privacy-first:

  • GPS coordinates are processed entirely on-device. The app uses iOS/Android geofencing APIs to detect when you enter a merchant's radius (approximately 150 m). The raw coordinates are never sent to our servers.
  • Geofence configurations (merchant name, category, latitude, longitude, radius) are stored in device-local storage only (AsyncStorage). They are not synced to our backend.
  • Only locations you explicitly pin are monitored. AutoPilot does not track your movement in the background beyond the specific geofences you configure.
  • You can disable AutoPilot at any time in Settings, which immediately stops all background location access.

2.6 Subscription and Payment

Mobile Web

Paid subscriptions (Pro, Max, Lifetime) are processed by Stripe. Rewardly stores:

  • Subscription tier and status — e.g. pro, active, renewal date.
  • Stripe customer ID — an opaque identifier (e.g. cus_abc123) that Stripe assigns to you. We use this to look up your subscription without storing payment details ourselves.
  • Billing period and provider — monthly, annual, or lifetime; payment platform (Stripe, Apple, or Google).

Stripe handles all payment processing. Rewardly never sees, transmits, or stores your credit card number, bank account number, or billing address. Stripe's data practices are governed by the Stripe Privacy Policy.

On mobile, subscriptions may also be processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store), governed by their respective privacy policies.

2.7 Achievements and Gamification

Mobile Web

We store which in-app achievements you have unlocked and when, associated with your user account. This is used to display your progress in the Achievements screen.

2.8 Card Applications and Referrals

Mobile Web

  • Card application tracking — if you use the 5/24 application tracker, we store the card name, application date, and status (approved, pending, declined).
  • Referral codes and signups — if you share your referral code, we track which referral code was used at signup to attribute referral credits. We record your referral code, the date of the referred signup, and aggregate referral counts.
  • Affiliate link clicks — when you click a "Apply Now" link for a card, we log the click (card identifier, timestamp) to track affiliate conversions. No personal identifiers are included in data sent to affiliate partners.

2.9 Usage Tracking

Mobile Web

We track feature usage counts (e.g. number of AI messages sent per billing period) to enforce free-tier limits and to understand which features are used most. This data is associated with your account but is aggregated and not shared externally.

2.10 Analytics

Web

The Rewardly web app uses Umami Analytics for privacy-respecting page-view analytics. Umami does not use cookies, does not track users across sites, and does not collect personal identifiers. Only aggregate page-view and session data is collected.

2.11 Chrome Extension Data

Extension

The Chrome extension stores data locally using Chrome's built-in storage APIs and does not require a Rewardly account. All of the following stays on your device (or, for items marked "synced," across your own Chrome browsers via Google's Chrome Sync):

  • Credit cards you own — card identifiers only (e.g. amex-cobalt). No card numbers, CVVs, expiry dates, or cardholder names. Stored in chrome.storage.sync.
  • Loyalty programs — program identifiers only (e.g. pc-optimum, scene-plus). No membership numbers or points balances. Stored in chrome.storage.sync.
  • Extension settings — blacklisted sites, popup preferences, notification settings. Stored in chrome.storage.sync.
  • Savings log — for each visit to a supported merchant: merchant name, spend category, recommended card, estimated reward value, page URL, and timestamp. Stored in chrome.storage.local on this device only.
  • Internal analytics log — extension events (popup shown, card recommendation displayed, etc.) with timestamps and page URLs. Used to power the in-extension savings dashboard. Stored in chrome.storage.local only.
  • Cached card and merchant data — a daily refresh of the card reward-rate database and merchant list, fetched anonymously from our backend. This is application data, not your personal data. Stored in chrome.storage.local.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ Bank account credentials, online banking usernames, or passwords
  • ❌ Credit or debit card numbers, CVVs, expiry dates, or cardholder names
  • ❌ Loyalty program membership numbers or points balances
  • ❌ Credit score, creditworthiness, or lending history
  • ❌ Raw GPS coordinates (location is processed on-device for AutoPilot only)
  • ❌ Individual shopping cart contents (product names, SKUs, or prices)
  • ❌ IP address or device identifiers beyond what Supabase Auth requires for session management
  • ❌ Cross-site tracking identifiers or advertising IDs
  • ❌ Data from third-party analytics, ad networks, or tracking pixels (beyond Umami on web, which is cookieless)

5. Third-Party Services

Supabase (database and authentication)

Rewardly uses Supabase for user authentication (Supabase Auth) and database storage (PostgreSQL). Your account data, card portfolio, transaction records, and AI chat history are stored on Supabase-managed infrastructure. All tables have Row Level Security (RLS) policies enforced so that users can only access their own data. Supabase's data handling is governed by the Supabase Privacy Policy.

Stripe (payments)

Stripe processes all subscription and one-time payments. When you check out, you are directed to a Stripe-hosted page. Rewardly receives only a Stripe customer ID and subscription status — never your payment details. Governed by the Stripe Privacy Policy.

Anthropic / OpenAI (AI)

Sage AI chat messages are processed by Anthropic (Claude) or OpenAI (GPT). Your message and card context are sent to these providers to generate a response. Please review their privacy policies: Anthropic Privacy Policy, OpenAI Privacy Policy. You can delete your Sage conversation history at any time from the app.

Google Places API

When you search for merchant locations to add to AutoPilot, the search query is sent to the Google Places API. This is an anonymous search — no account identifier is included in the request. Governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

Apple / Google (mobile platforms)

The iOS and Android apps are distributed through Apple and Google's app stores. Platform-level services (push notifications, geofencing, in-app purchases) are subject to Apple's and Google's privacy policies.

Chrome Sync (extension)

The Chrome extension uses chrome.storage.sync to sync your card selections and settings across Chrome browsers signed into the same Google account. This data is encrypted and managed by Google. Governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

Umami Analytics (web)

The web app uses Umami for cookieless page-view analytics. No personal data is collected. Aggregate session data only.

Card issuers (affiliate links)

When you click an "Apply Now" link, you are redirected to the card issuer's website. Rewardly may receive a referral commission for successful applications. The card issuer has their own privacy policy. We do not share your personal data with card issuers as part of this referral; the link contains only an affiliate tracking code.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Supabase-managed PostgreSQL databases. Key security measures:

  • Row Level Security (RLS) — database-level policies ensure that queries from authenticated users can only access their own rows.
  • Encrypted connections — all data in transit uses TLS/HTTPS.
  • Passwords — hashed by Supabase Auth (bcrypt). We cannot recover your password.
  • Location data — never transmitted to our servers. Stored only in device-local storage.
  • Payment data — never stored by Rewardly. Handled entirely by Stripe's PCI-DSS compliant infrastructure.

Despite these measures, no system is completely secure. We recommend using a strong, unique password for your Rewardly account and enabling two-factor authentication if available.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • Your profile, card portfolio, spending data, AI chat history, achievements, and all other personal records are deleted from our database.
  • Stripe retains billing records as required for financial and legal compliance (typically 7 years) — this is governed by Stripe's policies, not ours.
  • Anonymized, aggregate analytics data (e.g. total number of users who used Sage) may be retained, but this data cannot be linked back to you.

You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Account → Delete Account.

For the Chrome extension specifically: all data is stored on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the extension. We do not have backups because we never receive this data.

8. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights under applicable privacy laws (including PIPEDA in Canada and GDPR for EU/UK users):

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — update inaccurate data via Settings, or contact us.
  • Deletion — delete your account and all associated data (Settings → Account → Delete Account), or contact us.
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Withdrawal of consent — disable AutoPilot location access at any time in Settings; withdraw AI chat processing by not using Sage.
  • Objection — object to specific data processing by contacting us.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@rewardly.ca. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Rewardly is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us and we will delete the account promptly.
