Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

TL;DR: Rewardly stores your card selections, loyalty memberships, and shopping activity (merchant, category, page URL, and the estimated reward value of each transaction) locally on your device. We never send any of this to our servers. We never see your bank credentials, card numbers, or your identity. Card selections and settings sync across your own Chrome browsers via Google's built-in Chrome Sync. Your privacy is non-negotiable.

What Data We Collect

Rewardly collects the minimum data needed to provide its core service. All of the following is stored on your own device (and, for items marked "synced," across your own Chrome browsers via Google's Chrome Sync):

  • Credit cards you own: Card identifiers only — e.g., amex-cobalt or rbc-avion-visa-infinite. We never ask for and never store card numbers, CVVs, expiry dates, or cardholder names. Stored in chrome.storage.sync (synced across your Chrome browsers).
  • Loyalty programs you belong to: Program identifiers only — e.g., pc-optimum, scene-plus. No membership numbers, points balances, or personal details. Stored in chrome.storage.sync.
  • Extension settings: Your preferences (blacklisted sites, popup settings, notification preferences). Stored in chrome.storage.sync.
  • Savings log: For each visit to a supported Canadian merchant, the extension records the merchant name, spend category, the card it recommended, an estimated reward value derived from the cart total shown on the page, a checkout flag, the page URL, and a timestamp. Example entry: { merchant: "Loblaws", category: "groceries", cardRecommended: "amex-cobalt", estimatedSavings: 4.20, url: "loblaws.ca/cart", timestamp: ... }. Stored in chrome.storage.local on this device only.
  • Internal analytics log: Extension events (popup shown, card recommendation displayed, upgrade suggestion clicked, etc.) with timestamp, the URL of the page where the event occurred, and the user agent string of your browser. Used to power the in-extension savings dashboard. Stored in chrome.storage.local on this device only.
  • Cached card and merchant data: A daily refresh of the 393-card reward-rate database and 240-merchant list, fetched from our backend so you don't pay network costs on every checkout. This is application data, not your data. Stored in chrome.storage.local.

What We DON'T Collect

  • ❌ Bank account credentials or passwords
  • ❌ Credit card numbers, CVVs, expiry dates, or cardholder names
  • ❌ Loyalty program membership numbers or points balances
  • ❌ Your name, email address, phone number, postal address, or date of birth
  • ❌ Your credit score, creditworthiness, or lending history
  • ❌ Individual items in your shopping cart (product names, SKUs, descriptions)
  • ❌ IP address, GPS location, or any geolocation data
  • ❌ Cookies or cross-site tracking identifiers
  • ❌ Any of the above via third-party analytics, ad networks, or tracking pixels

Where Your Data is Stored

Rewardly uses Chrome's built-in storage APIs. We do not operate any user database or server-side store of your personal data.

  • chrome.storage.sync: Your selected cards, loyalty programs, and settings. This data is encrypted and synced by Google across Chrome browsers where you are signed in to the same Google account. Google's handling of sync data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.
  • chrome.storage.local: Savings log, internal analytics log, affiliate-suggestion state, and the cached card/merchant database. This data stays on this device only and is never uploaded anywhere.

We do not operate any backend that stores user data. The only server-side component Rewardly talks to is a public, read-only card-database endpoint (described below).

What We Do With Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide Rewardly's core features:

  • Show you the best credit card recommendation at checkout
  • Calculate your estimated savings over time for the in-extension dashboard
  • Suggest a different card from your wallet when it would consistently earn you more (only when the estimated monthly gain is at least $10)
  • Respect your blacklist and settings preferences

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers or data brokers
  • Use your data for cross-site advertising or retargeting
  • Build user profiles for marketing
  • Use or transfer your data to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes

Third-Party Services

Rewardly makes anonymous, read-only HTTPS requests to a Supabase-hosted public database (https://zdlozhpmqrtvvhdzbmrv.supabase.co) to fetch the latest credit card reward rates, reward programs, and point valuations. These requests:

  • Use a public anonymous API key — no user login
  • Contain no personal data of any kind in the request body, headers, or query string
  • Are issued roughly once per day by the background service worker for caching purposes
  • Return JSON data only — no remote code is ever loaded or executed

If you click a "learn more" or card-application link, you will be redirected to the card issuer's website (e.g., American Express, RBC, Scotiabank). Those sites have their own privacy policies — we recommend reading them before applying. Rewardly may be compensated for successful applications through affiliate relationships with card issuers; this does not change the recommendation algorithm, which always shows the card in your own wallet that earns the most.

Cookies and Tracking

Rewardly does NOT use cookies or any tracking technologies. We don't integrate with Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party analytics services.

Data Retention

Your data is stored locally until you:

  • Uninstall the extension (all data is deleted automatically)
  • Manually clear your Chrome storage
  • Remove specific cards or loyalty programs through the settings

We do NOT have backups of your data because we never receive it in the first place.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data by opening the extension popup → Settings
  • Delete: Remove individual cards/programs or uninstall the extension entirely
  • Export: Chrome's sync storage can be accessed via Developer Tools (for power users)

Security

Because all data is stored locally on your device, security is managed by Chrome's built-in protections. We recommend:

  • Keeping Chrome up-to-date
  • Using a strong device password
  • Enabling two-factor authentication on your Google account (if using Chrome sync)

Children's Privacy

Rewardly is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you're under 18, please do not use this extension.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at rewardly.ca/privacy with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the extension after changes means you accept the new policy.
