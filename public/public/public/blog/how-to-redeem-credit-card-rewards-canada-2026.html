How to Redeem Credit Card Rewards in Canada 2026

The same points can be worth 0.5¢ or 2¢+ each — depending on how you redeem. Here's how to maximize every loyalty program in Canada.

The Core Rule: Redemption Method Drives Value

Canadian credit card rewards programs offer wildly different values for the same points depending on how you redeem them. Gift cards from an online portal might give you 0.5¢ per point; booking a business class flight through the same program could give you 2.5¢ per point — 5x more value from the same points. The key is knowing which redemption method your program rewards and targeting it.

How to Redeem: By Program

Aeroplan Points (TD, CIBC, Amex transfers)

Redeem at aeroplan.com or Air Canada app. Book flights on Air Canada and 40+ Star Alliance partners. Value varies significantly — business class international is the sweet spot.

Economy North America12,500–25,000 pts one-way (~1–1.5¢/pt)
Economy Europe/Asia30,000–55,000 pts (~1–1.5¢/pt)
Business class (transatlantic)75,000–90,000 pts roundtrip (~2–3¢/pt)
Statement credit ("Shop the Way")0.5¢/pt — avoid
Gift cards via Aeroplan store~0.7¢/pt — below-average value

Best strategy: hold points until you have enough for international business class. Use Aeroplan Family Sharing to pool with partner.

American Express Membership Rewards (Cobalt, Gold, Platinum)

Redeem at amex.ca. Transfer to airline/hotel partners at 1:1 for best value. Cashback via statement credit is possible but low value.

Transfer to Aeroplan (1:1)Up to 3¢+/pt in business class
Transfer to Marriott Bonvoy (1:1.2)Variable — good for hotel redemptions
Travel credit (via Amex Travel portal)~1¢/pt — decent for flexibility
Statement credit0.5–0.8¢/pt — avoid
Gift cards~0.5¢/pt

Best strategy: transfer to Aeroplan when you have a specific flight in mind. Avoid statement credits entirely — the value loss is significant.

PC Optimum Points (PC Financial, Loblaw banner stores)

Redeem in-store at Loblaw-banner stores (Loblaws, RCSS, No Frills, Shoppers Drug Mart, Esso). 10,000 pts = $10 off groceries at checkout. Fixed rate, simple to use.

Grocery redemption at Loblaw/RCSS1¢/pt (10,000 pts = $10)
Shoppers Drug Mart1¢/pt on eligible purchases
Travel (via PC Travel)~1¢/pt — similar to grocery

Best strategy: redeem on Spend-It Events (usually 4x or 5x Shoppers events on specific Tuesdays). Redeem for groceries at RCSS for guaranteed 1¢/pt value. Don't let a large balance sit — points don't grow.

Scene+ Points (Scotiabank Gold, Scene+ Visa)

Redeem at scene.ca, at Cineplex, or at Sobeys/Safeway grocery stores. 1,000 pts = $10 at grocery; 100 pts = $1 movie ticket.

Grocery at Sobeys/Safeway/FreshCo1¢/pt ($10 = 1,000 pts)
Cineplex movie tickets1¢/pt (100 pts = $1)
Travel via Scotia Rewards~1¢/pt
Gift cards~0.7¢/pt — usually lower value

Best strategy: use at grocery for consistent 1¢ value. Cineplex is also 1¢ if you go to movies. If you're a Scotiabank Gold holder, travel redemptions via Scotia offer similar value to grocery without the retailer restriction.

Cashback Cards (Rogers Red, TD Cash Back, Tangerine)

Cashback is the simplest redemption — money automatically credited to your account or applied as a statement credit. Rogers Red credits monthly; TD Cash Back lets you redeem when you request it.

Rogers Red cashbackCredited monthly automatically
TD Cash Back redemptionRedeem $25 increments via EasyWeb
TangerineMonthly credit to your account

Cashback is the most flexible reward — it's real money, can be used anywhere, and doesn't require understanding a loyalty program. The trade-off is lower ceiling value compared to travel redemptions.

Common Redemption Mistakes to Avoid

Never redeem Amex MR or Aeroplan for merchandise or gift cards. These redemption options are offered at dramatically below-average rates — often 0.5¢/pt or less, compared to 1.5–3¢+ for travel. If you need cash value from points, statement credit (while still not ideal) is better than merchandise. Better yet, transfer to Aeroplan and book a flight.

Time redemptions around promotions: PC Optimum runs "Spend-It" events where you can earn 4x–5x points at Shoppers; Scene+ runs bonus point events; Aeroplan occasionally offers transfer bonuses from Amex MR. Timing large redemptions and earning sprees around these events can meaningfully increase value.
