PC Optimum Rewards Guide Canada 2026

PC Optimum is Canada's largest loyalty program — 30 points/$1 at Loblaws and Shoppers Drug Mart with the right credit card means effectively 4.5% back on your grocery and pharmacy bill.

What Is PC Optimum?

PC Optimum is the loyalty program of Loblaw Companies — Canada's largest grocery retailer. The program covers all Loblaws-banner grocery stores and Shoppers Drug Mart locations. Points are worth 1¢ per 100 PC Optimum points when redeemed in-store (10,000 points = $10 discount at checkout). Points can only be redeemed at participating stores — no travel, no cash transfer.

Program basics: Register free at pcoptimum.ca or via the PC Optimum app. Scan your registered card or app barcode at checkout. Combine with a PC Financial Mastercard to double-earn: loyalty points (from the app) + credit card points (from the card) on every purchase at Loblaws/Shoppers.

PC Optimum Bonus Events — When to Stock Up

1

Shoppers Drug Mart "Big Bonus Days" (Thursday–Sunday events)

SDM runs major point bonus events 4–8 times per year. During these events: 20x PC Optimum points on most purchases = effectively 20% back toward groceries. Stock up on household staples, over-the-counter medications, cosmetics, and vitamin supplements during Big Bonus Days.

2

Weekly In-App Personalized Offers

Every Thursday, PC Optimum app releases personalized weekly offers: 50x–2,000x points on specific products you've purchased before. These are individual to each member and highly variable. Check the app every Thursday and plan your grocery list around the best offers. Personalized offers often deliver 25–40% back on specific items during the offer period.

3

Mastercard Bonus Earn at Partner Retailers

PC Financial Mastercard cardholders occasionally receive extra points at non-Loblaws retailers (e.g., specific restaurant chains, travel partners, online merchants). Check the PC Optimum app and PC Financial account portal for current offers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use PC Optimum points at Shoppers Drug Mart to buy gift cards?
Shoppers Drug Mart has restrictions on PC Optimum redemptions: you cannot use points to purchase gift cards, lottery tickets, tobacco, alcohol, transit passes, or postage stamps. Points redemptions are applied to your eligible purchase total at checkout. Plan your SDM purchases accordingly — pick up household items, pharmacy products, beauty supplies, and OTC medications during point-redemption trips.
Do PC Optimum points expire?
PC Optimum points expire after 12 months of account inactivity (no earning or redeeming). Actively using your PC Financial Mastercard or scanning your PC Optimum app at any participating store resets the clock. Members who shop regularly at Loblaws or Shoppers Drug Mart will never experience point expiry. Check your expiry date in the PC Optimum app under "My Points."
Is PC Optimum better than Scene+ for grocery shopping?
They're equally valuable at 1¢/100 points — the question is which grocery chain you shop at. PC Optimum: Loblaws, No Frills, Real Canadian Superstore, Shoppers Drug Mart. Scene+: Sobeys, IGA, Safeway, FreshCo. PC Optimum has more store locations across Canada (Loblaws + SDM are the two largest grocery+pharmacy chains in the country), and PC Financial Mastercard earns slightly more than basic Scene+ card at grocery stores. For Canadians near both chains, many hold both loyalty programs and shop strategically based on bonus events.
