Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free checked bag benefit work?
When you book an Air Canada flight using your CIBC Aeroplan card, you and up to 8 companions on the same booking each get the first checked bag free (up to 23 kg). A family of 4 flying round trip saves $240 (4 people × $30/direction × 2 directions).
What counts as a "drugstore" for the 1.5× earn?
Shoppers Drug Mart, Rexall, Pharmasave, Guardian, I.D.A., and other pharmacies coded as drug stores (MCC 5912) in the Visa network. Note that some Shoppers Drug Mart purchases (like Food Basics groceries inside) may code differently.
Can I earn Aeroplan Status Qualifying Miles with this card?
Yes — you earn 1 Status Qualifying Mile (SQM) per $5 spent on the card, capped at a certain amount per year. SQMs count toward Aeroplan 25K, 35K, 50K, and Super Elite 75K status tiers, helping you reach elite status faster.
Should I get CIBC or TD Aeroplan Visa Infinite?
If you shop at Shoppers Drug Mart regularly, CIBC wins for the 1.5× drugstore earn. If you already bank with TD, the TD version makes sense for seamless credit management. For most people, the cards are nearly identical — pick whichever bank you prefer or has the better current welcome offer.
What's the income requirement?
$60,000 personal annual income, or $100,000 household income. You must also have a good credit score (generally 700+).
